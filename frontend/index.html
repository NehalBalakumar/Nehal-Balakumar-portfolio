Publications

Parametric Variation Analysis of FD-SOI MOSFET for High-Speed IC Design Applications

Proceedings Article · IEEE Conference · April 2025

Sukanya Ghosh · Santhosh Senthil · Dev Khetan · Nehal Balakumar

Traditional bulk MOSFETs suffer from severe short-channel effects in nanoscale regimes. This work presents a comprehensive analysis of Fully Depleted SOI (FD-SOI) MOSFETs using SILVACO ATLAS TCAD, focusing on variations in metal work function, source doping, top silicon thickness, and gate oxide thickness. Key results include a threshold voltage of 0.37 V and a subthreshold swing of 42.3 mV/dec, demonstrating suitability for high-speed IC design.
